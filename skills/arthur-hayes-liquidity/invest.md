# Arthur Hayes Liquidity

Crypto is a high-beta expression of global liquidity and risk appetite. Follow the money, the leverage, and the policy backdrop before falling in love with a narrative.

## Philosophy

Crypto is a high-beta expression of global liquidity and risk appetite. Follow the money, the leverage, and the policy backdrop before falling in love with a narrative.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: crypto.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's crypto competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal leverage/structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **dollarLiquidity** (high): Is global/dollar liquidity expanding or tightening?
- **riskAppetite** (high): Are investors embracing or rejecting speculative risk?
- **fundingLeverage** (high): Is leverage crowded, neutral, or washed out?
- **cyclePsychology** (medium): Euphoria, despair, or forced positioning?

## Filters

- **liquidityRegimeMapped** (required)
- **leverageContextKnown** (required)
- **narrativeOnlyNoLiquidity** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Map dollar liquidity and policy backdrop.
2. Check crypto leverage/funding and risk appetite.
3. Decide risk-on, neutral, or risk-off posture.
4. Express via BTC/ETH/beta with explicit invalidation.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Volatility-adjusted; avoid narrative domination.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden duplicated crypto beta.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: weekly.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, market/on-chain/research context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Risk-On / Neutral / Risk-Off / Probe / Reduce. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **Liquidity-driven crypto bull phases**: when expanding dollar liquidity and risk appetite lift BTC and beta assets.
- **Funding and leverage flush cycles**: crowded leverage creating violent but temporary dislocations.
- **Essay-driven macro-crypto framing**: connecting policy, liquidity, and crypto risk appetite.
