# Duan Yongping Benfen Value

做对的事情，然后把事情做对。投资就是买生意：好生意、好价格、好管理层，然后拿住。不懂的不做。

## Philosophy

做对的事情，然后把事情做对。投资就是买生意：好生意、好价格、好管理层，然后拿住。不懂的不做。

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: public equities.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's circle of competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **businessQuality** (high): Is it a good business with durable moat and pricing power?
- **understandability** (high): Can you explain the business model in one sentence?
- **priceVsValue** (high): Is the price right relative to what the business is worth?
- **managementIntegrity** (high): Are management honest and aligned with shareholders?
- **holdingConviction** (medium): Would you be comfortable holding if the market closed for 5 years?

## Filters

- **understandable** (required)
- **goodBusiness** (required)
- **rightPrice** (required)
- **honestManagement** (required)
- **dontUnderstand** (disqualifier)
- **badBusinessCheap** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Ask: do I understand this business? If not, stop.
2. Judge: is it a good business? Moat, pricing power, durable economics.
3. Check: is the price right? Is there margin of safety?
4. Verify: is management honest and aligned?
5. Decide: if all pass, own it and hold. If any fails, pass.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Constrained by model style and risk.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden concentration.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: event-driven.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Own / Watch / Too Hard / Too Expensive / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **NetEase investment**: buying a quality business at a depressed price during market fear, then holding for years as the business compounded.
- **Apple ownership**: understanding the ecosystem moat, brand pricing power, and user stickiness before the market fully priced it.
- **Step-by-step business building (BBK/Subor)**: the benfen principle applied to operations and investing alike — do the right thing, then do things right.
