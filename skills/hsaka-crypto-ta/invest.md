# Hsaka Crypto TA

In crypto, structure and invalidation matter more than prediction. Define the level that proves you wrong before you size the trade.

## Philosophy

In crypto, structure and invalidation matter more than prediction. Define the level that proves you wrong before you size the trade.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: crypto.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's crypto competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal leverage/structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **marketStructure** (high): Trend, range, or breakdown?
- **invalidationLevel** (high): Where is the trade clearly wrong?
- **leverageCrowding** (high): Is the market one flush away from forced selling?
- **narrativeAlignment** (medium): Does the story support the structure?

## Filters

- **structureClear** (required)
- **invalidationDefined** (required)
- **noStructure** (disqualifier)
- **blindLeverage** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Define market structure.
2. Mark invalidation.
3. Check leverage and narrative context.
4. Size only when risk is defined.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Volatility-adjusted; avoid narrative domination.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden duplicated crypto beta.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: daily.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, market/on-chain/research context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Long Setup / Short Setup / Wait / Overlevered Trap / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **Trend continuation after structure holds**: clean higher highs/higher lows with defined invalidation.
- **Leverage flush reversals**: crowded positioning wiped out before structure turns.
- **Narrative + structure alignment**: only acting when story and chart agree.
