# CryptoCred Structure

Good crypto TA is structured and falsifiable. Wait for confluence, define risk first, and avoid improvising after entry.

## Philosophy

Good crypto TA is structured and falsifiable. Wait for confluence, define risk first, and avoid improvising after entry.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: crypto.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's crypto competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal leverage/structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **keyLevels** (high): Support/resistance that matter.
- **structure** (high): Break, hold, or fail?
- **confluence** (high): Independent signals agreeing.
- **predefinedRisk** (high): Stop and size known before entry.

## Filters

- **levelsMapped** (required)
- **riskPredefined** (required)
- **singleIndicatorOnly** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Map key levels.
2. Read structure.
3. Require confluence.
4. Write entry/stop/invalidation before sizing.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Volatility-adjusted; avoid narrative domination.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden duplicated crypto beta.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: daily.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, market/on-chain/research context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Setup Valid / Needs Confluence / Wait / Invalid / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **Level + structure confluence**: multiple independent technical reasons align.
- **Failed breakouts with clear invalidation**: structure rejects and risk is defined.
- **Educational trade planning**: entry, stop, target, and invalidation written before execution.
