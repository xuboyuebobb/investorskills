# Muddy Waters Forensic Short

Management stories and audited numbers are hypotheses, not facts. Edge comes from primary evidence: documents, cash trails, channel reality, and related-party maps that the market has not fully discounted.

## Philosophy

Management stories and audited numbers are hypotheses, not facts. Edge comes from primary evidence: documents, cash trails, channel reality, and related-party maps that the market has not fully discounted.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: public equities, derivatives.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's circle of competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, weak evidence, fatal structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **documentInconsistency** (high): Filings, footnotes, and public claims do not reconcile.
- **channelRealityGap** (high): Distributor/end-demand checks diverge from reported growth.
- **relatedPartyWeb** (high): Insiders, affiliates, or off-balance entities distort economics.
- **cashVerification** (high): Cash, receivables, and working capital may not be real or accessible.

## Filters

- **primaryEvidence** (required)
- **mechanismSpecific** (required)
- **borrowSurvivable** (required)
- **headlineOnly** (disqualifier)
- **noFalsifiableClaim** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Map the market's trusted management story.
2. Identify the specific alleged false mechanism.
3. Gather primary evidence: filings, channel checks, related parties, cash trail.
4. Define catalyst, short structure, squeeze/time risk, and invalidation.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Constrained by model style and risk.
- **Cash / pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Acting without primary evidence or outside edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, evidence, or structure breaks.
- **Correlation**: Avoid one-factor concentration.
- **Leverage / short structure**: Only with explicit risk controls.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: event-driven.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, incentives/structure, risk budget.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Short / Watch / Evidence Incomplete / Structure Risk Too High / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **Sino-Forest-style complex structure shorts**: document and channel work challenging reported asset and revenue reality.
- **Channel and distributor verification cases**: testing whether end-demand matches reported growth.
- **Related-party and VIE complexity reviews**: mapping who actually controls cash, assets, and economics.

## Do's and Don'ts

- Do demand primary evidence.
- Do define the mechanism precisely.
- Do define invalidation.
- Don't trade narratives without proof.
- Don't ignore incentives and structure.
- Don't force a decision when data is incomplete.
