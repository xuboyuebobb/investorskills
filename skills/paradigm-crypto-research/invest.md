# Paradigm Crypto Research

In crypto, deep protocol research is edge. Understand the mechanism before underwriting the token or company.

## Philosophy

In crypto, deep protocol research is edge. Understand the mechanism before underwriting the token or company.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: crypto, private equities.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's crypto competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal leverage/structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **mechanismQuality** (high): Is the protocol design sound?
- **incentiveAlignment** (high): Do users, operators, and tokenholders align?
- **technicalMoat** (high): Is there durable protocol advantage?
- **researchEdge** (high): Do primary technical insights precede market pricing?

## Filters

- **mechanismUnderstood** (required)
- **primaryResearchReviewed** (required)
- **tokenFirstNoMechanism** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Read primary protocol research and docs.
2. Map incentives and attack surfaces.
3. Underwrite technical and economic moat.
4. Only then judge token/company expression.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Volatility-adjusted; avoid narrative domination.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden duplicated crypto beta.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: event-driven.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, market/on-chain/research context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Research Long / Watch / Mechanism Weak / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **Research-first protocol investing**: technical essays preceding market consensus.
- **Incentive and mechanism critiques**: understanding how protocol design creates or destroys value.
- **Infrastructure primitives**: L2, trading infrastructure, and crypto-native financial mechanisms.
