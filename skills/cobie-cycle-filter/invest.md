# Cobie Cycle Filter

Most crypto losses come from leverage, ego, and narrative capture. A good filter often means not doing the stupid thing.

## Philosophy

Most crypto losses come from leverage, ego, and narrative capture. A good filter often means not doing the stupid thing.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: crypto.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's crypto competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal leverage/structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **cycleTemperature** (high): Fear, greed, or mania?
- **narrativeFragility** (high): Is the story load-bearing or decorative?
- **leverageEgoRisk** (high): Are size and confidence too high?
- **invalidationClarity** (high): Do you know when you are wrong?

## Filters

- **invalidationExists** (required)
- **sizeSurvivable** (required)
- **pureFomo** (disqualifier)
- **noExitPlan** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Judge cycle temperature.
2. Stress-test the narrative.
3. Check size, leverage, and ego.
4. Stand down if the trade depends on not being wrong.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Volatility-adjusted; avoid narrative domination.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden duplicated crypto beta.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: event-driven.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, market/on-chain/research context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Proceed / Reduce / Stand Down / Narrative Trap / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **Late-cycle FOMO filters**: when everyone is confident and leverage is high.
- **Narrative trap avoidance**: stories that sound smart but have no invalidation.
- **Survival over brilliance**: staying in the game through cycle violence.
