# Multicoin Thesis VC

Crypto rewards clear theses. Identify where value accrues, why the market structure is changing, and whether the token or equity can capture it.

## Philosophy

Crypto rewards clear theses. Identify where value accrues, why the market structure is changing, and whether the token or equity can capture it.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: crypto, private equities.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's crypto competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal leverage/structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **sectorThesisClarity** (high): What structural shift is being underwritten?
- **valueCapture** (high): Who gets paid as the network grows?
- **executionPath** (high): Can builders ship the world the thesis assumes?
- **marketStructureEdge** (medium): Is this a new market form or a copy?

## Filters

- **thesisExplicit** (required)
- **valueCaptureClear** (required)
- **buzzwordOnly** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Write the sector thesis in one paragraph.
2. Map value capture to token/equity.
3. Test execution and adoption path.
4. Size only when thesis and capture both clear.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Volatility-adjusted; avoid narrative domination.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden duplicated crypto beta.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: event-driven.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, market/on-chain/research context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Thesis Invest / Watch / Weak Value Capture / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **High-performance chain theses**: betting where throughput and UX unlock new crypto applications.
- **Token value capture frameworks**: distinguishing useful networks from unownable public goods.
- **Sector concentration**: high-conviction thematic exposure rather than index-like VC.
