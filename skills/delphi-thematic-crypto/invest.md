# Delphi Thematic Crypto

Crypto themes move in waves. Map the sector, understand token incentives, and time the narrative before it becomes consensus entertainment.

## Philosophy

Crypto themes move in waves. Map the sector, understand token incentives, and time the narrative before it becomes consensus entertainment.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: crypto.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's crypto competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal leverage/structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **sectorMapClarity** (high): Do you understand the whole theme stack?
- **tokenomicsQuality** (high): Are incentives sustainable?
- **narrativeStage** (high): Early, expanding, or late?
- **researchDepth** (medium): Primary research vs headline chasing.

## Filters

- **sectorUnderstood** (required)
- **tokenomicsReviewed** (required)
- **lateNarrativeOnly** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Map the sector and key projects.
2. Review token incentives and value capture.
3. Judge narrative stage.
4. Allocate to theme leaders or wait.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Volatility-adjusted; avoid narrative domination.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden duplicated crypto beta.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: weekly.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, market/on-chain/research context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Overweight Theme / Single-Name Research / Watch / Narrative Late / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **Sector map investing**: understanding a theme before picking names.
- **Tokenomics quality control**: separating sustainable designs from emissions games.
- **Narrative timing**: entering themes before full retail saturation.
