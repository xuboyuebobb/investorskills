# Willy Woo On-Chain

Price is the last thing to move. On-chain supply, holder behavior, and realized value often show the cycle before headlines do.

## Philosophy

Price is the last thing to move. On-chain supply, holder behavior, and realized value often show the cycle before headlines do.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: crypto.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's crypto competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal leverage/structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **holderBehavior** (high): Accumulation or distribution by cohorts.
- **realizedValue** (high): Where is aggregate cost basis?
- **supplyDynamics** (high): Illiquid supply, exchange balances, issuance.
- **cyclePosition** (high): Early, mid, late, or reset?

## Filters

- **onchainDataAvailable** (required)
- **multipleMetricsAgree** (required)
- **singleMetricMagic** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Map holder cohorts and exchange supply.
2. Compare price to realized/on-chain cost basis.
3. Diagnose cycle position.
4. Decide accumulate, hold, distribute, or wait.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Volatility-adjusted; avoid narrative domination.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden duplicated crypto beta.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: weekly.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, market/on-chain/research context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Accumulate Zone / Distribute Zone / Neutral / Data Incomplete / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.

## Canonical Cases

- **On-chain accumulation phases**: long-term holders absorbing supply while price is quiet.
- **Distribution and euphoria phases**: realized profits and supply returning to exchanges.
- **Cycle metric dashboards**: combining multiple on-chain signals instead of one magic number.
