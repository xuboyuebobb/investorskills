# a16z Crypto

Crypto is an ecosystem, not a single trade. Underwrite where adoption is forming and whether the company or network can become foundational.

## Philosophy

Crypto is an ecosystem, not a single trade. Underwrite where adoption is forming and whether the company or network can become foundational.

## Universe & Regime

- **Asset classes**: crypto, private equities.
- **Universe**: Opportunities inside this model's crypto competence.
- **Preferred regime**: Where the edge is active.
- **Avoid**: Outside competence, missing data, fatal leverage/structure.
- **Posture**: Evidence-first and risk-aware.

## Signals

- **adoptionMetrics** (high): Users, developers, transactions, retention.
- **stackPosition** (high): Infra, mid-layer, or app?
- **openNetworkLeverage** (high): Does openness create compounding distribution?
- **founderEcosystemFit** (medium): Can the team shape a category?

## Filters

- **adoptionPathClear** (required)
- **stackRoleClear** (required)
- **tokenNarrativeOnly** (disqualifier)

## Analysis

1. Locate the project in the crypto stack.
2. Measure adoption and developer traction.
3. Judge whether openness and incentives compound.
4. Choose equity/token expression carefully.

## Position Sizing

- **Initial size**: Only after filters pass and risk is defined.
- **Add rule**: Add as evidence improves.
- **Max size**: Volatility-adjusted; avoid narrative domination.
- **Cash/pass rule**: Valid when no setup clears the bar.

## Risk Management

- **Primary risk**: Applying the model outside its edge.
- **Invalidation**: Thesis, structure, or risk-budget break.
- **Correlation**: Avoid hidden duplicated crypto beta.
- **Leverage**: Limited unless model explicitly supports it.

## Execution

### Entry Rules

1. Inside universe.
2. Core signals support action.
3. Filters pass.
4. Risk and invalidation defined.

### Exit Rules

1. Thesis breaks.
2. Evidence reverses.
3. Risk budget breached.
4. Better opportunity appears.

## Monitoring

- **Cadence**: event-driven.
- **Metrics**: Core evidence, risk budget, market/on-chain/research context.
- **Alerts**: Invalidation triggers and missing critical data.

## Playbooks

### Decision Memo

Return one of: Ecosystem Invest / App Invest / Infra Invest / Too Early / Pass. Include thesis, evidence, risks, action plan, and invalidation.
